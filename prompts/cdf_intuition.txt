An educational step-by-step diagram showing how a CDF (Cumulative Distribution Function) is built. Left panel: a histogram of ages (20-50) with bars. Right panel: the same data as a smooth S-shaped cumulative curve going from 0% to 100%. An arrow connects them labeled "Sort and accumulate". The Y-axis on the right is labeled "% of people below this age". A dotted horizontal line at 50% meets the curve, drops down to the X-axis showing the median. Clean educational style, blue color scheme, white background, large readable labels.
